===================================
PhD Student Computer folder structure
===================================

This directory structure is designed to organize the files and data for a PhD student's computer. Here's how it's organized:

01. Documents: Contains folders for personal,adminstrative, grants, courses and literature. 
02. Projects: Contains folders for your protocols and projects (which has folders for experiments, data, analysis and results)
03. Presentations: holds presentations for lab meetings, conferences and retreats, etc...
04. Thesis: holds the drafts for your thesis and defense preparation.
05. Manuscript: holds the drafts and figures for your manuscripts.
06.README.txt: This file :) 

Feel free to adapt this structure to your specific needs and workflow. Keeping your files organized will help streamline your 
research process and make it easier to find and access important information.
